Famous Monuments of India

Taj Mahal, Agra

A symbol of love built by Shah Jahan for Mumtaz Mahal, known for its white marble beauty and Mughal architecture.

Qutub Minar, Delhi

World’s tallest brick minaret, showcasing Indo-Islamic architecture with detailed carvings and verses.

Hampi, Karnataka

The remains of the Vijayanagara Empire, a UNESCO World Heritage Site known for its temple complexes and stone structures.

Konark Sun Temple, Odisha

Famous for its chariot-shaped structure dedicated to the Sun God, representing the peak of Kalinga architecture.

Gateway of India, Mumbai

Iconic arch monument built during the 20th century to commemorate King George V and Queen Mary’s visit to India.

Amber Fort, Jaipur

Magnificent hilltop fort known for its artistic Hindu-style architecture and panoramic views of Jaipur city.
